Special terms and conditions for category Emporium Products 

1.This product category has been specially created on GeM for Direct and PAC buying of handloom products,
handicrafts products and gift articles etc. The procurement under this category is limited to RS 50,000/- for
Direct buying and unlimited value for PAC buying. By uploading any product under this category, the seller
undertakes that there is no predefined category available on GeM for the offered item. While making
procurement under this category, Buyer also has to undertake that category of item selected is not otherwise
available on GeM for competitive procurement. Any procurement from this category in violation of these
guidelines will render the contract so concluded as null and void. Since there are no defined parameters to
compare the products of different OEMs/ Sellers, Buyer has to be doubly sure about reasonableness of price of
the product being procured. Any false claim by seller about product specifications and about rate having been
approved by any government agency will be treated as a Severe Deviation under the Incident Management
Policy of the GeM.

Bid Specific Additional Terms and Conditions
1.Procurement under this bid is reserved for purchase from Micro and Small Enterprises from the State of Bid

Inviting Authority whose credentials are validated online through Udyog Aadhaar for that product category. If the
bidder wants to avail the reservation benefit, the bidder must be the manufacturer of the offered product in case
of bid for supply of goods. Traders are excluded from the purview of Public Procurement Policy for Micro and
Small Enterprises. In respect of bid for Services, the bidder must be the Service provider of the offered Service.
Relevant documentary evidence in this regard shall be uploaded along with the bid in respect of the offered
product or service.
