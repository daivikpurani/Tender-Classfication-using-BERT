Special terms and conditions for category Laying & Installation Charges for Cables (with
or without conduit) Suitable for CCTV System(Accessor 

1. In addition to General Terms and Conditions of the GeM following Special T&C shall be
applicable for Laying & Installation charges for Cables (with or without conduit) suitable for
CCTV System:

1. Inclusive in the Scope of laying & installation:
1.Supply of Conduit and Laying of Conduit with necessary fittings such as

bends, joints, reducers, Tees etc., wherever required.
2.All necessary accessories such as conduit pipe fittings, mounting clips etc.,

for proper laying and installation of Conduit, wherever required.
3.Supply of suitable Connectors required for connecting the cable with Camera

/ DVR / NVR / HVR / SFP module / PoE Switch etc.
4.Proper dressing of Cable ends, Pre-forming of connecting cables (end

termination) and Fixing suitable connectors.
5.Digging of trench for underground laying and properly closing the trench

after completion of laying work, wherever required.
6.The trench for underground laying, wherever applicable, may be upto 30 cm

(1 foot) wide and depth as specified in the contract. seller may use horizontal
boring machine if required.

7.Cutting / Chiselling of grooves in the walls for concealed laying in walls and
Cementing & finishing of the grooved portion after completion of laying work,
wherever required.

2.Not - inclusive in the Scope of laying & installation:
1.Obtaining necessary approvals / permissions from the concerned Authorities,

if any, for Digging trenches, Cutting / Chiselling of grooves in the walls,
wherever required.

3.Buyer's obligations:
1.Obtaining necessary approvals / permissions from the concerned Authorities,

if any, for Digging trenches, Cutting / Chiselling of grooves in the walls,
wherever required.

2.Buyer / Consignee shall provide power point for drilling holes, cutting
grooves, etc., for indoor laying & installation of cables.
