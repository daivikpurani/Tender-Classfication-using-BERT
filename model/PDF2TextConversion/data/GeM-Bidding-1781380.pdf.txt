Special terms and conditions for category Photocopier Paper with Printing 

1.Subject to provisions of Public Procurement (preference to `Make in India`) order 2017
notified vide Department of Industrial Policy and Promotion Ministry of Commerce and
Industry order dated 31st July2018 the following types of Paper Paper Board and Newsprint
where sufficient domestic manufacturing capacity and local competition is available
minimum local content is specified as below: Type of Paper - Photocopier Paper with Printing
Relevant BIS Standard - IS 14490:1997 Minimum Local Content (%) - 75

2.The minimum local content shall be reckoned with reference to ex-factory price (pre-GST) on
which the manufacturer has paid GST.

3.The Public Procurement of paper for office supplies and printing of textbooks should be
certified to be manufactured in India by paper mill that does not import more than 25% of its
total raw material consumed.
