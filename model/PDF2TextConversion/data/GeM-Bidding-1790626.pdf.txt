1.Special Terms and Condition for Personal Protection Equipment (PPE) Body Coverall for Covid 191. Only
manufacturers having valid certificate indicating UNIQUE CERTIFICATION CODE (UCC) for Personal Protection
Equipment (PPE) Body Coverall for COVID-19 from DRDO/OFB/SITRA labs authorized by Ministry of Textiles
should upload their products. No re-sellers/traders are allowed to upload the products in this category.2. Buyers
are advised to check the authenticity of the UCC code from the websites of DRDO/OFB/SITRA labs authorized by
Ministry of Textiles. The suggestive links arehttps://drdo.gov.in/personal-protective-equipment-ppe-body-coverall-
certificate-listhttps://ofb.gov.in/pages/ucc-issuedhttp://www.sitra.org.in/However, appropriate links may be
verified by the buyers.3. Buyers are advised to follow a post delivery quality check in terms of MOHFW guidelines
dated 2 nd March
2020[https://www.mohfw.gov.in/pdf/GuidelinesonrationaluseofPersonalProtectiveEquipment.pdf] and Ministry of
Textiles letter No F. No 8/4/2020-R&D dated 16-04-2020[http://texmin.nic.in/sites/default/files/9.Textiles-
PPEBodyCoverallsTestCertAddlProc16Apr2020.pdf ]. The procedures enumerated at Para-3 and 4 of the
instructions of Ministry of Textiles, Govt. of India issued vide letter No. F.No. 8/4/2020-R&D dated 6th April,
2020[http://texmin.nic.in/sites/default/files/6.Textiles-QC_Letter-06Apr2020.pdf] must be strictly complied.4. The
OEMs should mandatorily upload the certificate as mentioned at Clause 1 above while submitting the bid in case
of / at the time of supply in case of direct supply without bidding.For more detailes please refer to this
link.https://gem.gov.in//resources/pdf/stc-for-ppe-coverall-for-covid-19.pdf
